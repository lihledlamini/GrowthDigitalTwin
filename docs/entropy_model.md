# Entropy Model

## Introduction

Entropy represents the natural loss of usable energy within the human system.

GrowthDigitalTwin uses entropy as a measurement of disorder, inefficiency, and energy leakage.

---

# Sources of Entropy

Examples:

- Stress
- Poor sleep
- Distractions
- Procrastination
- Illness
- Conflict
- Excessive screen time
- Mental fatigue

---

# Entropy Effects

High entropy can cause:

- Reduced focus
- Lower productivity
- Poor decision making
- Reduced recovery

---

# Entropy Reduction

Entropy can be reduced through:

- Rest
- Exercise
- Meditation
- Planning
- Healthy routines
- Recovery activities
