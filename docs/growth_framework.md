# Growth Framework

## Introduction

GrowthDigitalTwin defines human growth as a multidimensional system composed of interconnected domains.

The three primary dimensions are:

1. Spiritual Growth
2. Physical Growth
3. Mental Growth

---

# Spiritual Growth

Spiritual growth represents the development of purpose, values, discipline, and inner balance.

Features:

- Prayer
- Meditation
- Reflection
- Gratitude
- Purpose
- Compassion
- Service
- Emotional stability

Output:

Spiritual Growth Score

---

# Physical Growth

Physical growth represents biological health and physical capability.

Features:

- Sleep
- Exercise
- Heart rate
- Steps
- Nutrition
- Recovery
- Fitness level
- Body measurements

Data sources:

- Smart watches
- Fitness trackers
- Manual input

Output:

Physical Growth Score

---

# Mental Growth

Mental growth represents cognitive development and psychological performance.

Features:

- Learning
- Reading
- Programming
- Research
- Creativity
- Focus
- Memory
- Stress management

Output:

Mental Growth Score

---

# Interaction Between Domains

The three domains influence each other.

Example:

Poor sleep may reduce:

- Mental performance
- Emotional control
- Spiritual discipline

Therefore growth must be modeled as an interconnected system.
